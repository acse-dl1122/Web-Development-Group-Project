This is one of my favorite holiday events. I am bummed that I won't be going this year due to the pandemic, 
but I'm hoping we can all celebrate next year. The actors do a great job of staying in character and the vendors 
are equally delightful. The entire experience will convince you that you are in a magic world. There are fun 
shows too. See them all. I've been a few times and still feel like there is more to see. Decent variety of 
worldwide food and drink and above all - good cheer!
